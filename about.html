Our Story

How we're revolutionizing the real estate industry with AI technology

Pioneering the Future of Real Estate

Founded in 2020, Real Estate AI began with a simple vision: to harness the power of artificial intelligence to simplify and enhance the property experience for everyone. Our team of real estate experts, data scientists, and technology innovators came together to create a platform that transforms how people buy, sell, and invest in property.

What started as a small startup in San Francisco has grown into a leading real estate technology company serving clients across the United States. Our proprietary AI algorithms analyze millions of data points to provide accurate valuations, predictive market trends, and personalized property recommendations.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of clients and partners, helping them make smarter real estate decisions with confidence. Our journey continues as we develop new technologies to make real estate transactions more transparent, efficient, and accessible to everyone.
